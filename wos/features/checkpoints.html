Checkpoints

Checkpoints are self-explanatory. This page will go over all the extra features around checkpoints in WOS.


Animations

WOS's animated checkpoints feature is a fun and creative tool that allows users to customize the appearance of checkpoints. With a variety of animation styles to choose from, including classic, spin, world, and color-only, you can tailor the checkpoints to match the look and feel of your game. Once a player touches the checkpoint, the animation or color of the checkpoint changes according to your settings. This feature is a great way to add an extra layer of engagement and excitement to your game, making it more dynamic and immersive. The animated checkpoints feature is a great way to add some personality and flair to your game.


Sound Effect

The sound effect that is played when a player reaches a new checkpoint. If there is no ID in Configurations, no sound effect will play.


Checkpoint Color

The custom checkpoint color (ReachedCheckpointColor) is the color the checkpoint turns after a player reaches it.


Included in version 0.8.
