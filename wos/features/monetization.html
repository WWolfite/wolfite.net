Monetization

Monetization in WOS contains of the classic monetization features found in most obbies. All monetization features are optional. If you wish to disable one, leave the ProductId/GamepassId at 0. Keep in mind WOS does not have a built-in store in 0.8.


Skip Stage

A skip stage product allows player to spend robux to skip a stage. This is a one time purchase and can be made multiple times. Keep in mind, every 40 minutes a user is
given a free skip, a free alternative to paying for a skip.


Type: Product


Easy Mode

Easy Mode allows a player to touch damage bricks and kill bricks without being affected.


Type: Gamepass


Coils

Coils are tools that give players special abilities. For example, a gravity coil will allow a player to jump higher while they equip it. There are 4 types of coils that are currently available in WOS: Gravity Coil, Speed Coil, Fusion Coil (bot gravity and speed), and the Heal Coil.


Type: Gamepass


Setup

This section will cover how to setup monetization features. This section will assume you know how to make a gamepass and a developer product.


Skip Stage

First of all, to make setup the skip stage product, you must have the developer product you want to use ready.
Next, go to the Configurations module and scroll down until you see the SkipStageID value. Then copy the ID of your developer product and paste it as the
SkipStageID value.


Gamepasses

Every other monetization feature is a gamepass. Under the SkipStageID value in the Configurations module are all the Gamepass values. You can match them with their respective gamepass IDs.

Included in version 0.8.*
